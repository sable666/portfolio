About Me

I am a geographer and GIS analyst with a strong foundation in web mapping, geospatial analysis, and data visualization. My work specializes in leveraging tools like ArcGIS, QGIS, and Leaflet to create engaging and informative digital maps. I hold a Master of Arts in Geography and a Graduate Certificate in Web Mapping from the University of Kentucky, as well as a Bachelor's degree in Geography with a minor in International Security and Conflict Resolution.

My professional experiences span urban forestry initiatives, park operations, and teaching assistance, where I honed my skills in spatial data management, environmental stewardship, and community engagement. I bring over 15 years of leadership and customer-facing experience, which enhance my adaptability and problem-solving capabilities.

Passionate about the intersection of geography, technology, and community, I aim to apply geospatial technologies to solve complex challenges, such as enhancing urban environments and supporting sustainable land management. My portfolio reflects a commitment to creating impactful and innovative solutions in the geospatial field.
